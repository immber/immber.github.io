About

I’ve spent the last 4 years owning my own software consulting practice and doing development on the side. Finally, I took a leap and decided to go full programmer. I just graduated from a 19 week intensive coding boot camp and am ready to figure out what’s next.

In past lives I’ve run wholesale distribution companies, grown startups, and been an accountant. Now what? I’m pretty sure that the next line of my story reads “and then she moved to San Francisco and became a web developer”.

Prior to attending Dev Bootcamp I developed some handy software utility applications designed for bookkeepers and accountants. They help you clean up data in QuickBooks, and work with all the desktop versions. You can check out my products at www.QBUtility.com. I also did some custom dev work for clients that had a very specific need to get something into or out of their QuickBooks company file.

Want to find out more about my consulting practice? Check out www.OutgrowingQuickBooks.com.

Curious about other ways that I fight boredom and make money? You should ask me about some of my other sites, and side projects.
