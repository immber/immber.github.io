About Jessie

I started JR Consulting because I realized that I had a unique range of experience, and that my expertise could be a value to growing businesses.

Many years ago, I started my career working in a warehouse packing and shipping orders. I saw firsthand how to, and how NOT to manage inventory. From there I worked my way up to Operations Manager and that’s where I learned how to make all of the parts of a puzzle fit together.

As a math geek, I naturally gravitated towards accounting, and since I was working in smaller organizations, that meant learning QuickBooks inside and out. I eventually earned a BS in Accounting, and continue to appreciate the beauty of perfectly balanced general ledger.

The further I got into accounting though, the more I became fascinated by the technology that drives the everyday transactions. As a software consultant I’ve worked with ERP, MRP, CRM, and WMS systems, and I still can’t get enough of the IT stuff.

At JR Consulting we unite the functions of Operations, Accounting, and IT. Our unique approach is how we make growing businesses thrive.
